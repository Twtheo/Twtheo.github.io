About Me

My name is Thomas Theodorescu and I am currently working as an Associate Software Development Engineer at Charles Schwab. I graduated from Colorado State University in the Spring of 2017 with a Bachelors Of Science in Computer Science and a Minor in Mathematics. I was born in Toronto, Canada and moved to the United States when I was only a few months old. My love of computers stems from my father and I spending hours building computers for our household.
